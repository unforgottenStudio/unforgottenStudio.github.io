A method for adults to acquire a second language by self in first language circumstance using information technology

Objectives: The emphasis here is on self-learning. If there is a teacher who can teach foreign languages like mothers teaching native language, of course, it is a different way. Usually practical conditions limit us to live in a native language environment and want to learn foreign languages by ourselves. As long as the method is correct, and with the help of information technology, we can also get good results.

We are using information technology to help people to accumulate memories and improve learning efficiency.

We have developed an algorithm that statistics the data of remembering process , and gets the principle of memory for everyone. Then we provide products that use smart devices (such as: smart phones) to remind people to review the knowledge. and use social networks to share knowledge.
